Built for local conditions

Tumkur water is hard.
We fix it at the source.

Most homes and businesses in and around Tumakuru run on borewell water, and in many areas that water is hard and high in dissolved salts (TDS). That's exactly what leaves white scale on your taps, makes water taste flat, and quietly shortens the life of geysers, washing machines and motor pumps.

A plain UV purifier can't remove dissolved salts or hardness — only an RO system does. For the borewell water common across the district, an RO or RO+UV purifier with a copper or alkaline stage is usually the right call for drinking water, while a water softener on the main inlet protects the whole building's plumbing and appliances.

That mix of RO purification and softening is most of what we install across Tumkur every week — for individual homes, apartments, schools, hostels, hotels and factories. We can test your water, tell you honestly what it needs, and stand behind the system afterward with service and annual maintenance.

Do you service water purifiers of all brands? +

Yes. We repair and maintain RO and UV water purifiers of all major brands across Tumkur and nearby towns — including units you didn't originally buy from us. Filter changes, membrane replacement, leaks and low water flow are all routine for us.

Which water purifier is best for borewell water in Tumkur? +

For borewell water with high TDS — common across Tumkur — an RO purifier (usually RO+UV with a copper or alkaline stage) is best, because it removes dissolved salts and hardness that UV-only purifiers leave behind. We can test your water's TDS first and recommend the exact model that suits it, rather than over-selling.

How often should RO filters be changed? +

As a rule of thumb, sediment and carbon filters every 6–12 months and the RO membrane every 2–3 years, depending on your water quality and daily usage. Harder water shortens these intervals. For AMC customers we track and remind you, so it's never missed.

Do you offer AMC / annual maintenance contracts? +

Yes — we offer annual maintenance contracts for both domestic and commercial RO systems. Filters, servicing and priority breakdown support are handled on a fixed schedule so your water stays safe year-round.

How much does a commercial RO plant cost? +

It depends entirely on the capacity you need (litres per hour) and your source water — a hostel and a packaged-water unit need very different plants. Call us for a free site assessment and a clear written quote with no obligation.

Do you install solar water heaters, and do they actually save money? +

Yes. A solar water heater is a one-time investment that cuts your water-heating electricity cost for years afterward. We handle sizing (LPD), rooftop fitting, plumbing and the electrical backup connection, plus later service and descaling.

Which areas around Tumkur do you cover? +

Tumakuru city and across the district — including Gubbi, Kunigal, Tiptur, Sira, Madhugiri, Koratagere, Chikkanayakanahalli, Turuvekere and Pavagada. If your town isn't on our list, call anyway — we travel for installations and commercial work.
